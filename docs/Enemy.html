Class: Enemy

Enemy

new Enemy(health, x, y, speed, damage, detectionRadius)

This is the constructor for the most general type of enemy. All future enemies will have the properties and methods that are declared here.
Parameters:
Name Type Description
health Number The amount of damage the monster can take before dying
x Number The initial x-position of the enemy
y Number The initial y-position of the enemy
speed Number How fast the enemy is going to move
damage Number How much damage the enemy does per attack
detectionRadius Number The "view distance" of the enemy. When a fighter is within this radius, the enemy will chase them

attack(enemy, player)

Called when a player collides with the enemy. Enemy's attack Animation is played and the player that is colliding takes an amount of damage defined in the constructor.
Parameters:
Name Type Description
enemy Sprite The enemy that is one half of the collision.
player Sprite The player that is the other half of the collision

update(playerGroup)

Makes the enemy chase the closest player to it at any given time. There is a certain range that if the player is outside, the monster just walks around randomly.
